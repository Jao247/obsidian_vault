# Cinnamon Apple Snack

**1 Serving**

---
- *2x* Apples, Cut in slices
- *60g* Greek Yoghurt
- *10g* Peanut Butter (runny consistency if made from powder)
- *1 tsp* Cinnamon
- *Drizzle* of Maple Syrup
---
# Method:
1. Add Ingredients to Container
2. Shake the shit out of it
3. Enjoy!
